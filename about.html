About Us

Welcome to R-Tech, your number one source for all things technology related. We're dedicated to giving you the very best service, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness.


Founded in 2020 by James R., R-Tech has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office, Whangarei, Northland. When James first started out, his passion for technology and helping others drove him to quit his day job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming service business. We now serve customers all over Northland, and are thrilled to be a part of the innovative wing of the IT industry.

We hope you enjoy our service as much as we enjoy offering it to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
James R., Owner, Operator
